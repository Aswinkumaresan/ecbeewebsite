B2B2C Ecommerce

Ecbee, E-Commerce portal allows the business owners to unlock the potential growth opportunities for their
product or brand by combining the B2B and B2C business models.

Our B2B2C supports the Business owners to reinvent their enterprise brand with the B2B2C Model

How B2B2C Business Model transforms your brand

Give a winning edge to your enterprise business by reaching out to B2B as well as B2C buyers

---------

Setup your brand for growth, like never before with the B2B2C business model.

A perfect model to utilise your existing dealer and distribution network to build your brand and to reach the new customer base.

-------------------------

New online customers orders are directed to the local distributor to promote the brand as well as the loyalty with the existing dealer network. This B2B2C ensures cost of logistics is reduced along with quicker to deliver the product to the end customer.

Encouraging the existing business network to participate and be part of various business clusters, to gain more advantage by means of increased gain or other benefits. This creates a healthy business relationship with our network by means of instant gratification.

----------------

Our Ecbee, e-commerce platform provides solution of having multiple logistics options that leverages both state level and pan India logistics network. This logistics solution ensures the product reaches the customer in time and without damage on any fragile items, the customer has order for.

This B2B2C solution combines the best of B2B and B2C to reach out to new buyers and sellers. This model helps you to design business strategies for no-holds-barred growth.
