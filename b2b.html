B2B Ecommerce

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Our Ecbee, Ecommerce platform ensures, supports B2B Solutions to customers meeting their specific need in their businesses like supply large quantity of raw materials, components or finished goods to be used by other business customers.
  • Our platform supports tailor made solutions for B2B Customers, who deals with large order volumes, complex contracts in their business with their business partners/customers, which varies from business to business, Our platform ensures such complex requirements are met without any manual intervention leading to hassle free operations of B2B business.
  • Our portals which we develop through our E-Commerce platform provides solution for B2B customers who have customized pricing, collaborative working methodology with existing dealer or distribution network. Our indigenous technology platform allows the customers for efficient communication to enhance growth prospect of their business dealing with B2B customers.

Why ECBEE for B2B Ecommerce Solutions

One of the challenges faced in B2B is to handle multiple scenario contracts for various needs of the business customers. Our E-commerce platform
ensures the contractual obligations are met and the day-to-day operations are carried over with ease and hassle-free business environment.

Order Processing

Our platform makes sure the orders received are processed in time and the users can efficiently manage the orders with easy and manageable user interface that helps the order processing progress from order received to delivery of goods to the customers in time. The portal which we develop through our E-commerce platform handles repeated orders (from history). Our E-commerce technology platform supports with solutions for the B2B customers to run their business with multiple scenario promotions, discount based on quantity order, the total invoice amount, clustering high value business customer/partner which help in their business growth and build business strategy to promote the products or their brands. Our platform helps the B2B customer to identify their high value customers and cluster them using different parameters.

B2B payment Management

Our E-commerce platform allows Business owners to define the payment process for their clients. Clients with established credit terms will have the options to raise purchase order without getting directed to the payment process, and also allows the business owners ensuring their clients are not crossing the pre-set credit limits to safeguard the business. Our E-commerce platform is so compatible in such a way that, it can be integrated to any payment API and other API of your business applications like Inventory, POS and accounting systems.

Promotion Management

Our E-commerce platform is designed in such a way that the customer can have multiple promotions for their customers to promote their products or brands. The various types of promotions now available are customer promotion, category promotion, order promotion, product promotion, registration promotion etc. The customer has the preference to come up with their new promotion ideas which can also be implemented through our business compatibly designed E-commerce platform. Options are available for promotion hierarchy and precedence.
