Hybrid D2C Online Marketplace

D2C commerce ensures the brand reaches the end users directly and provides avenue to build loyalty and brand value. It also helps the manufactures to directly sell their products to the direct users minimising their operational cost. D2C approach in E-Commerce redefines the department of Commerce with an alternate approach and unleash the faster growth.Sell products directly to the end customers through a network of dealers located in multiple states and cities. Build your hybrid D2C online marketplace where your customers buy on your marketplace website or app. Fulfil the orders using your existing dealer network by routing the orders through the nearest dealer from the customer location.

Our Ecbee E-Commerce platform, D2C solution free the direct manufacturers brand from the dependencies and limitations of traditional direct retail selling. D2C solution help the manufacturers to redesign their strategies for growth like never before.
