D2C Ecommerce

Go D2C in no time with tailor-made solutions that give you full control over your brand

Whatever Be Your D2C Strategy StoreHippo Has A Built-In Solution For Your Brand

D2C Online Marketplace

Direct To Consumer Model With Brand Dealers as Sellers

Sell wholesale products directly to other businesses by digitising your sales channels. Create your brand’s D2C online marketplace to sell in bulk to end customers without involving middlemen. Onboard your dealers as vendors to enable your customers to compare, choose and buy from the dealer of their choice.

Hybrid D2C Online Marketplace

D2C Model With Fulfillment Through Brand Dealers

Sell products directly to the end customers through a network of dealers located in multiple states and cities. Build your hybrid D2C online marketplace where your customers buy on your marketplace website or app. Fulfil the orders using your existing dealer network by routing the orders through the nearest dealer from the customer location.

D2C Horizontal Marketplace

Direct-To-Consumer Model Selling Brand Products Along With Ancillaries From Other Sellers

Sell your branded products along with ancillary products to the end customers. Build your horizontal D2C online marketplace to sell your brand’s products as well as onboard different sellers selling supplementary products related to your industry.
