Features

Simple and empowering features of StoreHippo multi-vendor marketplace

Seller Self-Registration

SQuick and easy vendor registration allows vendors to register with complete details. Unique vendor account is created for each vendor by filling details in a simple registration form. Complete details of vendor company, address, login, bank details etc. captured in one go.

Approval Flow

Admin has full control to approve or reject the products uploaded by the vendors. Products uploaded by vendors can be displayed on marketplace only after approval from the admin thus applying a check on any unwanted product uploads

Vendor Dashboard

Separate Vendor dashboard for each vendor gives easy understanding of inventory and order status. Vendor gets a snapshot of his business using the dashboard which provides order summary, sorting functionality, sales report, best selling products, revenue details and much more.

Product Management

Allow easy management of products by vendors wherein vendors can add, edit, upgrade products by logging in. Give them the authority to add and view complete inventory, product variants, product details, pricing image etc. Upload products in bulk using CSV.

Admin Dashboard

Business owner gets a complete overview of everyday business by logging in the admin panel. Get the fine details of every aspect of your business like sales, revenue, vendor performance, bestselling products etc. to help you better business management.

MACH Architecture

Leverage MACH architecture for ecommerce to build your disruptive multi vendor marketplace. Easily scale to new markets, sellers, product lines and customer groups by powering your online marketplace with the scalability, reliability, agility and flexibility of the MACH (Microservices, API-first, Cloud-Native, Headless) architecture for ecommerce..
